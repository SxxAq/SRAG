
Employees are entitled to 18 annual leaves, 12 casual leaves, and 10 sick leaves per year.
Annual leaves can be carried forward up to a maximum of 30 days. Casual and sick leaves
cannot be carried forward.

Unapproved leaves lead to salary deductions as per the HR compliance rules. Employees
must apply for leave at least 2 days in advance except in medical emergencies. A medical
certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.
